About Me

I am currently working as a algorithm engineer at Alibaba Group, where I focus on using Causal Inference and Uplift Modeling to recommend the best promotion method for merchants.

Before that, I obtained my master degree from Zhejiang University in 2023, fortunately advised by Yang Yang, and bachelor degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology in 2020.

My research interests lie within Time-Series Data Mining and Graph Data Mining, especially in Self-Supervised Learning and Domain Adaptation. After joining Alibaba, I became interested and started researching on Causal Inference.
