About Me

I am currently employed as a machine learning engineer at Alibaba Group, where my primary focus revolves around leveraging Causal Inference and Uplift Modeling to customize promotional tool configurations for merchants operating on the e-commerce platform.

Prior to this role, I successfully completed my master's degree at Zhejiang University in March 2023, with the privilege of being advised by Yang Yang. I also earned my bachelor's degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology in June 2020.

My research interests primarily encompass Time-Series Data Mining and Graph Data Mining, with a special emphasis on Self-Supervised Learning and Domain Adaptation. Following my association with Alibaba, I developed a keen interest in Causal Inference.

I am actively in pursuit of a Ph.D. opportunity, with a target start date in the fall of 2024.
